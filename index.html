A little bit of heaven on a secluded acre

Brand new in December 2025, Itty Bitty Acres is a beautiful cottage nestled on a quiet no-through road in Verrierdale, in the heart of the Noosa Hinterland. Surrounded by nature yet only 15 minutes to the beach and 20 minutes to Noosa Heads.

Whether you’re after a romantic couples getaway (1-bedroom setup), a family escape (2 or 3 bedrooms), or just pure peace and quiet — this is your perfect Noosa Hinterland base. Every guest so far has left a 5-star review.
